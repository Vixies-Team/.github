# Vixies Team

Welcome to **Vixies Team**!

Vixies Team is an independent creative and technology-focused organization from Indonesia, founded by a group of university students who share a passion for learning, building, and creating together.<br>
Our goal is to provide a place where ideas can grow into real projects. Through collaboration, experimentation, and continuous learning, we work on a variety of initiatives ranging from software development and game creation to community-driven activities.

## Our Philosophy

We believe that the best way to learn is by creating.

Every project, whether large or small, is an opportunity to gain experience, improve our skills, and explore new ideas. We value curiosity, teamwork, creativity, and continuous improvement.
